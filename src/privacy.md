# Privacy

Ardent Leatherworks LLC collects only what is necessary to fulfill orders and communicate with customers.

## What We Collect

**When you place an order:**
- Name and shipping address
- Email address
- Payment information (processed by Stripe, never stored by us)

**When you contact us:**
- Email address
- Message content

## How We Use It

- To fulfill and ship your order
- To communicate about your order
- To respond to questions
- To send updates if you requested them

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Third Parties

**Stripe** processes payments. Their privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy

**Cloudflare** hosts this site and provides security. Their privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy

## Data Retention

Order records are kept for tax and warranty purposes. Contact us to request deletion of personal data not required for legal compliance.

## Contact

Questions about privacy: contact@ardentleatherworks.com
